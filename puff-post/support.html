Support

Quick help for Puff Post on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

The camera is unavailable

Choose Demo Route from the home screen at any time. To restore motion play, allow camera access for Puff Post in system Settings, then return to Start Delivery.

Motion control pauses

Move back into the camera’s view with your shoulders and hand visible. Puff Post pauses safely during tracking loss, so it will not drop a parcel or deduct delivered points.

Music or sound is missing

Check Settings → Background Music and Game Sound Effects. On iPhone and iPad, Puff Post also respects the system Ring/Silent switch.

The screen looks cropped

Puff Post uses landscape orientation on iPhone and iPad. Turn Rotation Lock off and relaunch the app. Include your device model, operating system version, and the app version shown on the About page when contacting support.

Contact

Email liwali.app@gmail.com with your device model, system version, app version/build, and a short description. Do not attach camera images or other sensitive personal information.

《摇摇云邮车》技术支持

相机不可用

你可以随时从首页选择“演示路线”。如需恢复体感游玩，请在系统设置中允许 App 使用相机，再回到“开始送信”。

体感控制暂停

请回到镜头范围内，并让肩膀和手部清楚可见。追踪中断时游戏会安全暂停，不会因此掉包或扣除已送达分数。

没有音乐或音效

请检查“设置 → 背景音乐”和“游戏音效”。在 iPhone 和 iPad 上，App 也会遵守系统响铃/静音开关。

画面显示不全

iPhone 和 iPad 使用横屏。请关闭竖排方向锁定后重新打开 App。联系支持时请提供设备型号、系统版本及“关于”页面显示的 App 版本。

联系方式

请发送邮件至 liwali.app@gmail.com，写明设备型号、系统版本、App 版本与构建号及问题描述。请不要附加相机画面或其他敏感个人信息。
