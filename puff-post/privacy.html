Privacy Policy

Puff Post works without an account, advertising, analytics, or cloud storage.

Camera and motion processing

If you choose motion control, Puff Post uses the device camera only during motion setup and gameplay. Apple Vision processes each frame on the device to estimate body or hand position and produce a temporary left-or-right control value.

Puff Post does not save, upload, share, or retain camera frames, body landmarks, pose coordinates, or motion history. Capture stops when you leave motion setup or gameplay, when a route ends, or when the app enters the background. The Demo Route works without camera access.

Data collection

Puff Post does not collect personal data or usage analytics. It stores only local preferences and counters on the device, including onboarding completion, music and sound settings, and the successful-delivery count used to limit review prompts. These values never leave the device.

Network access and third parties

The app does not require a network connection and includes no advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, social, or tracking SDKs. It does not sell or share personal data or track users across apps or websites.

Children

Puff Post is a general-audience game and is not enrolled in the App Store Kids category. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other user.

Your choices

Camera permission can be denied or revoked in system settings. The camera-free Demo Route remains available. Background music and sound effects can be disabled independently in Settings.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, email liwali.app@gmail.com.

App 不会保存、上传、分享或保留相机画面、人体关键点、姿态坐标或动作历史。离开体感准备或游戏、路线结束、App 进入后台时，相机采集会停止。演示路线无需相机权限。
